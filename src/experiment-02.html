Sol Lewitt
Wall Drawing #33, 1970

In my research to select the wall drawing I would code I realized that Sol Lewitt often specified the material that the draw should be made of, in this case colored pencil.

Rarely do I attempt to directly copy the original artwork. Instead, I prefer to learn by subverting it. For some time now, I've wanted to explore the p5.brush library, and I realized this would be an good opportunity to do so.
